Hello Nice to meet you and welcome to my little site to show you some pics of Tokyo and share some of my original music with you. I live in Tokyo. My name is Darren. I am from canada. I am an energetic, cheerful and hard-working English teacher. I live in Tokyo. I have taught English in Korea, China, Taiwan and now Japan for over 22 years. Before teaching English, I taught music in Canada. I also really enjoyed teaching music. However, teaching English in Japan is even more fascinating and a good learning experience for me. It's not just about Japan's wonderful culture and delicious food. From teaching English to children to teaching business English to adults, these experiences have given me a lot of ideas, materials and teaching skills. Grammar, pronunciation, daily conversation, TOEIC, business English, Eiken, etc. I will help you improve your English skills.
